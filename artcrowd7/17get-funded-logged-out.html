The ArtCrowd Process

Build an artist profile to exhibit your work with ArtCrowd. Create experiences and rewards to offer art patrons through a crowdfunding campaign. Use our unique Vernissage mode to create an invitation only campaign to find out what your patrons like, then use that feedback to take it world wide, Permanently exhibit your successful campaign artwork in the ArtCrowd Museum and offer selected rewards in our Museum Shop. Use your get paid and create more art!

We take you step by step, from building a profile, to designing a campaign, to installing your successful campaign in our ArtCrowd Museum and Museum Shop

1

Create your campaign

Follow the easy, step-by-step process to get your campaign up and running.

Create a profile as an artist or art professional. Upload your artworks to your profile gallery.

Create a campaign title and set an attainable funding goal for something you want to accomplish. Add documentation and images to tell your story.

Create interesting rewards and experiences that you can offer patrons who fund your campaign.

2

Vernissage

An ArtCrowd exclusive process. Boost your funding and chances for success with a private opening. Find out what works before you take the campaign public to the world.

Add the Vernissage mode to your crowdfunding campaign, offer some Vernissage only rewards for your special patrons.

Invite your selected patrons to try exclusive access to your campaign for a limited time.

Use their funding choices and comment feedback to edit and improve your campaign for worldwide launch.

3

Launch your campaign

Take your campaign public to reach a worldwide audience and get funded

Launch your campaign and make some noise, use the built-in links to share your campaign and individual rewards in email and through your social media.

Respond to feedback, comments and questions, your prospective patrons want to hear from you.

Create campaign updates and special mid campaign rewards to create more excitement as the campaign approaches its deadline and it’s funding goal.

4

You Made It!

Congratulations, you have now completed a successful funding campaign on ArtCrowd. Your payment from ArtCrowd will arrive within 15 days of conclusion.

Time to get busy. You have patrons eagerly awaiting their rewards, Give them feedback and let them know how progress on their rewards is coming and when they can expect it.

Add your campaign artwork to the ArtCrowd Museum, curate your story and select rewards that you would like to offer after the campaign has ended through the ArtCrowd Museum Shop.

Fulfill rewards. Bask in praise and gratitude from your happy patrons.

Get busy. Share on ArtCrowd your new creative progress and show your patrons what they helped create.
